About the author

Aida Sol

Wellness writer

Aida Sol is a wellness writer who believes the body speaks in patterns and hair is one of its clearest messengers. Drawing on years of research into nutrition, lifestyle, stress, and holistic wellbeing, she's discovered that what happens on your scalp rarely stays on your scalp. It reflects everything happening within. Through Healthy Happy Hair, Aida shares a thoughtful, accessible guide to nurturing stronger hair from the inside out. Rather than another product-focused book, she explores the real connections between how you care for yourself and what your body and hair reflects back. Her writing combines evidence-based wellness principles with practical strategies you can actually implement because lasting change comes from curiosity, consistency, and a kinder relationship with yourself.
