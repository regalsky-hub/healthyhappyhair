NERVOUS SYSTEM
& HAIR SIGNALS

Your nervous system governs everything — including your hair follicles. Chronic stress diverts blood flow away from the scalp, disrupts hormones, and can push follicles into early shedding. Learning to read your body’s signals is one of the most powerful things you can do for your hair.

COMMON STRESS-HAIR SIGNALS TO WATCH
Increased shedding Scalp tightness Sudden dryness Itching / tingling Slower growth Brittle strands Greying shifts Oilier scalp Edges thinning

SCALP
CARE GUIDE

A healthy scalp is the foundation of healthy hair. Think of it as the soil — what you grow depends entirely on how well you tend to the ground.

SCALP CARE ESSENTIALS
Cleanse regularly — remove product build-up, sebum and debris that block follicles.
Massage weekly — increases blood circulation to follicles. Even 3 minutes matters.
Hydrate from within — a dry scalp often reflects dehydration before it reflects product needs.
Protect your edges — avoid tight styles, friction and harsh chemicals around the hairline.
Feed your follicles — iron, zinc, biotin and omega-3 directly support scalp health.
