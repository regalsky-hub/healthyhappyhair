Healthy Happy Hair · 04

NERVOUS SYSTEM
& HAIR SIGNALS

Your nervous system governs everything — including your hair follicles. Chronic stress diverts blood flow away from the scalp, disrupts hormones, and can push follicles into early shedding. Learning to read your body's signals is one of the most powerful things you can do for your hair.

Common stress-hair signals to watch
Increased shedding
Scalp tightness
Sudden dryness
Itching / tingling
Slower growth
Brittle strands
Greying shifts
Oilier scalp
Edges thinning

Healthy Happy Hair · 05

SCALP
CARE GUIDE

A healthy scalp is the foundation of healthy hair. Think of it as the soil — what you grow depends entirely on how well you tend to the ground.

Scalp care essentials
Cleanse regularly — remove product build-up, sebum and debris that block follicles.
Massage weekly — increases blood circulation to follicles. Even 3 minutes matters.
Hydrate from within — a dry scalp often reflects dehydration before it reflects product needs.
Protect your edges — avoid tight styles, friction and harsh chemicals around the hairline.
Feed your follicles — iron, zinc, biotin and omega-3 directly support scalp health.

Healthy Happy Hair · 07

HOW TO USE THIS JOURNAL

Your hair tells the truth your body cannot hide. This journal is your quiet, honest companion — a place to notice what your hair and scalp are telling you, and to follow your progress from the roots over three months.

Before you begin
Read Healthy Happy Hair or keep it close — this journal follows the same structure.
Complete your Hair Goals & Vision page first — return to it at the end of each month.
Take a baseline photo before you begin so you can see your journey clearly.
How the months work
01Each month opens with a divider — read the intention, then dive in.
02Wash Day Logs — fill in after each wash. Two per month keeps it manageable.
03Nutrient Planners — use bi-weekly to track your hair-loving foods and supplements.
04Mind–Body Check-ins — bi-weekly. Your mood, sleep and cycle shape your hair more than you think.
05Monthly Reflection — close each month with an honest review. Patterns live here.
A gentle reminder

"Small, consistent entries beat perfect ones. Observe without judgment. Your hair is listening to how you treat yourself."

Healthy Happy Hair is shared for educational purposes only and is not medical or professional advice.
